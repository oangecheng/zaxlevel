为了增加游戏的后期成长性，因此制作了这个mod
(mod还在开发迭代中，很多细节还有待优化，欢迎大家提意见，目前游戏没做双语，后面再支持)

角色升级
1. 开启角色升级，所有角色的三维会被重置为 100 100 100
2. 战斗升级，击杀或者破坏对方(蜘蛛巢也行)获得对方最大血量的经验，一次最多只升一级
3. 吃特定的几种肉类食物升级，具体是哪些食物，游戏中体验吧（获得经验时会有提示）

角色强化
1. 击杀4000点血量以上的敌人，可以获得角色能力强化
2. 每次强化角色伤害+1%，防御+1%
3. 伤害强化上限50%，防御强化上限30%


种田能力提升(种菜是刻在骨子里的基因，游戏里面也一样，哈哈哈...)
1. 通过收获巨大作物提升自己的农业能力
2. 等级越高，升级需要收获的大作物数量就越多，1级需要1个，2级2个，3级3个，以此类推
3. 目前仅支持肥料使用效率提升，每20级提升肥料效率一倍，肥力上限100
4. 后面还会有其他扩展，敬请期待...


额外采集
1. 没有开启角色升级功能时，额外采集固定30%概率双倍掉落，无其他掉落规则
2. 开启角色升级时，角色等级越高，双倍采集的概率越高，初始20%，最高60%
3. 三倍采集概率为双倍采集的1/10
4. 0.5%的概率触发幸运事件，10倍掉落


额外掉落
1. 击杀血量1000及以上的敌人可以提升额外掉落的概率
2. 双倍掉落初始概率30%，最大50%
3. 三倍掉落概率为双倍掉落的1/10


武器强化
1. 可以使用物品提升武器的攻击力，最大使用次数，移动速度
2. 耐久为0不消失，攻击力降为0，使用金块恢复耐久，每次恢复 20%或者100次耐久(取最大值)
3. 因为武器攻击力提升很变态，因此这里采用了一个很昂贵的策略，铥矿棒+0.5，触手尖刺+0.02，攻击力上限80
4. 武器初始移速加成固定10%，可以使用海象牙提升移速，每次+1%，移速上限50%
5. 使用龙蝇鳞片提升最大使用次数，每次增加 100，最大使用次数上限2000
6. 和能力勋章联动（比较喜欢玩这个模组），给予高级伐木勋章和高级矿工勋章可以使武器具备挖矿和砍树能力，多给几个可以提升对应能力
7. 目前支持长矛、战斗长矛进行强化


帽子强化
1. 可以使用物品提升帽子属性，防水、保暖、精神恢复
2. 需要使用眼球解锁防水升级能力，使用猪皮可以提升防水，每个猪皮+1%，上限100%，
3. 需要使用熊皮解锁升级能力，可以使用冬象鼻提升保暖，每次+20，无上限，
4. 需要使用沙之石解锁升级能力，可以使用夏象鼻提升隔热，每次+20，无上限，
5. 通过使用红宝石和蓝宝石切换帽子形态，使用红宝石切换成隔热模式，蓝宝石切换为保暖模式，
   需要对应模式解锁了升级能力才能切换到对应模式，小心切换之后切不回来了
6. 需要使用暗影心脏解锁升级能力，可以使用海象帽升级，精神恢复每次+1，
7. 提醒：帽子耐久为0后会消失，记得修补哦
8. 目前仅支持海象帽强化

其他
1. 帽子强化可以通过设置选项“概率升级”，开启之后升级帽子属性有一定概率失败，属性越高，失败的几率越大(这里的策略还需要细化，目前写的比较粗糙)

In order to increase the later growth of the game, this mod was created.
This mod does not support English at the moment, it will be supported later.
(The mod is still under development and iteration, and many details have yet to be optimized. Comments are welcome)
(My English is not very good, please bear with me if there is a problem with the translation)

Role upgrade
1. Turn on character upgrade, all character attributes will be reset to 100 100 100.
2. Through combat upgrades, kill or destroy the opponent (also the spider nest) to gain the experience of the opponent's maximum HP, and only one level up at a time.
3. Eat certain kinds of meat food to upgrade, what kind of food is, experience it in the game (there will be a prompt when you gain experience)

Role reinforcement
1. Kill enemies with more than 4000 HP to get character ability enhancement
2. Each time the character is strengthened, the damage +1%, the defense +1%
3. The upper limit of damage enhancement is 50%, and the upper limit of defense enhancement is 30%


Farming ability improvement
1. Improve your farming abilities by harvest giant crops
2. The higher the level, the more large crops need to be harvested for the upgrade, 1 for level 1, 2 for level 2, 3 for level 3, and so on
3. At present, only the improvement of fertilizer use efficiency is supported, and the fertilizer efficiency is doubled every 20 levels, and the upper limit of fertility is 100
4. There will be other extensions later, so stay tuned


Additional pick
1. When the character upgrade function is not enabled, an additional 30% chance of being collected will be doubled, and there are no other pick rules.
2. When character upgrade is enabled, the higher the character level, the higher the probability of double pick, the initial 20%, the maximum 60%
3. The probability of triple pick is 1/10 of double pick
4. 0.5% chance to trigger lucky event, 10 times pick

Additional drop
1. Killing enemies with HP of 1000 and above can increase the probability of additional drops
2. Double the initial drop probability of 30%, maximum 50%
3. Triple drop chance is 1/10 of double drop

Weapon Enhancement
1. You can use items to increase the attack power, maximum number of uses, and movement speed of the weapon
2. If the durability is 0, it will not disappear, and the attack power will be reduced to 0. Use a gold nugget to restore the durability, and each recovery will be 20% or 100 times of durability (whichever is the maximum value)
3. Because the weapon attack power is very abnormal, a very expensive strategy is adopted here, ruins_bat +0.5, tentaclespike +0.02, and the maximum attack power is 80
4. The initial movement speed bonus of the weapon is fixed at 10%. You can use walrus tusks to increase the movement speed, +1% each time, and the maximum movement speed is 50%.
5. Use dragon fly scales to increase the maximum number of uses, each time increases by 100, and the maximum number of uses is 2000
6. Linked with Functional Medal (I prefer to play this mod), giving Advanced Logging Medal and Advanced Miner Medal can make the weapon capable of mining and tree cutting, and giving a few more can improve the corresponding ability
7. Currently support spear, spear_wathgrithr for strengthening

Hat Reinforcement
1. You can use items to improve hat attributes, waterproof, warm, and spiritual recovery
2. You need to use the eyeball to unlock the waterproof upgrade ability. Use pig skin to improve waterproof, each pig skin +1%, the upper limit is 100%,
3. You need to use the bear skin to unlock the warm pgrade ability, you can use the winter elephant trunk to improve the warmth, +20 each time, no upper limit,
4. You need to use the sand stone to unlock the upgrade the heat insulation ability, you can use the summer elephant trunk to improve the heat insulation, +20 each time, no upper limit,
5. By using red_gem ​​and blue_gem to switch hat form, use red_gem ​​to switch to heat insulation mode, and blue_gem to switch to warm mode,
   You need to unlock the upgrade ability in the corresponding mode to switch to the corresponding mode. After switching carefully, you cannot switch back.
6. You need to use the shadow heart to unlock the spiritual recovery upgrade ability, you can use the walrus hat to upgrade, the spirit recovery +1 each time,
7. Reminder: The hat will disappear after the durability is 0, remember to repair it
8. Currently only supports walrus hat enhancement
